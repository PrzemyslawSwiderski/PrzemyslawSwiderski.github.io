The day kicked off with a session on Amazon Bedrock, a powerful tool for evaluating and selecting foundation models for AI applications. The speaker emphasized the importance of evaluation to balance quality, cost, and latency trade-offs when choosing models.

Key Takeaways ðŸ”—
